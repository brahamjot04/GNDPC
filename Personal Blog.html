PERSONAL BLOG

My Name is Brahamjot Singh. I am currently studying Diploma in Computer Science Engineering at Guru Nanak Dev Polytechnic College, Ludhiana. I have completed my 10th from Guru Nanak Public School, Sarabha Nagar, Ludhiana with 79%. I love to listen to music and play video games. I have keen interest in Web Development and am currently learning it at Science and Technology Entreprenuers' Park (STEP-GNDEC) during the 6 weeks Industrial Training. I have the knowledge on how to work on Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Premiere Pro. I know how to speak, read and write Punjabi, English and Hindi.
I can easily learn something new easily, am good at Time Management and can Multitask easily. Sometimes, I get easily distracted by small things.
